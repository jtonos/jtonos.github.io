I a currently a graduate student in Amy Dunhams Tropical Ecology lab at Rice University. My work is focused on increasing our understanding patterns of interactions between individual plants and their frugivorour partners and their influence on plant community spatial structure and biodiversity. In my future work I hope to apply a theoretical understanding of the mechanisms and outcomes of these essential plant-animal interactions towards improved management, conservation and restoration of natural tropical communities.
